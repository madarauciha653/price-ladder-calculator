What is a Price Ladder Calculator?

A Price Ladder Calculator helps Binance traders visualize profit and loss across a range of buy prices. Instead of calculating one price at a time, you can see 21 price levels at once — 10 above and 10 below your base buy price.

How to Use This Tool

  • Enter your Base Buy Price — the price you plan to buy at
  • Enter your Buy Amount — how many coins you are buying
  • Enter your Sell Price — the price you plan to sell at
  • Select a Step Size — controls price intervals in the ladder
  • Click Generate Ladder to see your profit table

Binance Fee Included

This calculator automatically includes the standard Binance trading fee of 0.075% for both buy and sell orders, giving you accurate net profit results.

Who Is This For?

This tool is perfect for Binance spot traders, crypto scalpers, and anyone who wants to plan their trades more accurately by seeing profit at multiple price points before placing an order.
